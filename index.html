What is AP Chemistry?

Advanced Placement Chemistry is a rigorous and demanding college-level course. It is equivalent to a college introductory chemistry course. It is designed for students with an interest in science, medicine, engineering or a related field. Advanced Placement Chemistry is laboratory-oriented, with a substantial amount of home study required. This course prepares students for the Advanced Placement Examination.

How does AP Chemistry work at SBHS?

At SBHS, we place an emphasis on hands-on learning. You will have the opportunity to participate in labs and compile lab reports, even during virtual learning. You will also be able to work on practice problems, quizzes, chapter tests, and/or lab presentations during this course.

Which exams will this course prepare me for?

AP Chemistry will primarily prepare you for the CollegeBoard AP Exam for Chemistry. This Exam is designed to assess the skills and knowledge you will have developed during this course. You may also choose to take the SAT Chemistry Subject Test, which also tests your skills and knowledge in advanced-level Chemistry. More information about both of these exams can be found in this site.

Which resources will I be provided with during this course??

As you study for AP Chemistry, you will be given many resources to help you succeed in understanding the material. You will have a textbook which contains instructional and practice material. You will also be given an OWL Account, which generates online practice question sets, and a lab manual to assist with labs. You will always be able to reach out to your teachers, Dr. DeBari and Mr. Burnett, for help.

What Chemistry Related Extracurriculars does SBHS offer?

Chemistry Olympics is a team which competes in chemistry-related events ranging from nomenclature quizzes to research activities to even web design and molecule building! Science Olympiad is a team which participates in competitions with various concentrations, including Chemistry. Science League offers competitive tests for many concentrations including Chemistry I and II. Information for all three teams can be found on this site.

Science Olympiad

A team which lets you compete in multiple STEM events ranging from biology and chemistry to anatomy and computer science.

More Info

About

In Science Olympiad, you will be able to compete with a partner in multiple events that range from Chemistry, Physics, Biology, Computer Science, and Inquiry. Events may require you to build a device, take a test using notes, or a combination of the two. Your team score is calculated based on rankings in 26 events. You will take part in 2-4 events.

Competitions

Each year, the Science Olympiad team travels to and competes in the Yale University Invitational, Princeton University Invitational, and University of Pennsylvania Invitational. The Scioly teams also competes at the NJ Regional and State Competitions.

How to Join

Tryouts will be held in September to early October. Please email sbhsscioly@gmail.com with any questions.
